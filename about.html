Our Mission

At Dam-Lore, our mission is to create the ultimate guide for exploring the OSU campus and its surrounding areas. Unlike generic tools like Google Maps, our map is crafted specifically for OSU visitors—students, parents, and anyone eager to discover the heart of campus life. We go beyond the basics, highlighting hidden landmarks and cherished spots that often go unnoticed, complete with rich historical context gathered by our team.

We're also redefining the orientation experience. Say goodbye to cluttered, mismatched paper maps and confusing pamphlets. Our streamlined, all-in-one map is thoughtfully designed with custom icons, intuitive layouts, and detailed information to make navigating OSU simple, engaging, and memorable. With Dam-Lore, you're not just finding your way—you're discovering the true spirit of OSU.
