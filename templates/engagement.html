Join Our Community

Dam-Lore is a community-driven project that aims to document and preserve the rich history of OSU campus and the Corvallis area. We believe that the best historical collections are built through collaboration and shared knowledge from people who love their community.

Suggest a Landmark

Did we miss your favorite landmark? Help us expand our collection by suggesting new locations to add to Dam-Lore. Your contributions help make this resource more valuable for everyone!

What Happens Next?

1

Review

Our team reviews your suggestion to ensure it meets our guidelines and contains accurate information.

2

Research

We conduct additional research about the landmark to gather historical details and interesting facts.

3

Addition

The landmark is added to our database and becomes available on both the map and lore pages.

Thank you for helping us expand Dam-Lore! Your contributions make this project better for everyone in the OSU and Corvallis community.
