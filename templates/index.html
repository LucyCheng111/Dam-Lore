What is DAM Lore?

DAM Lore provides users a few features that makes it simple and fun to explore the OSU and Corvallis Areas.

We as the DAM Lore Team hope you enjoy this walk through our wonderful campus and town and learn more about the place we call home!

The Lore Page

Our collection of entries we thought every student and parent should see when they're in town.

Explore Lore

The Map Page

An easy way to find all locations you may want to visit from our entries. It includes a search bar, a custom map api, and detailed information panels for each landmark including historical facts, photos, and visitor tips.

View Map

The Itinerary Page

Looking at all entries can be overwhelming. Luckily, we have provided you with just the solution. Our itinerary page allows the user to choose from a selection of tours like the campus or culture tour to guide their Corvallis experience.

Plan Your Tour

The About and Mission Pages

These pages are dedicated to giving the user more information about our lovely team and a deeper explanation of our mission.

Our Team Our Mission

The Engagement Page

This project is from the students for the students, and with that we want to make sure it can evolve as the student body does. The engagement page focuses on collaboration with each student and user that visits this page and allows you, the user, to leave comments on potential areas of interest we may have missed.
