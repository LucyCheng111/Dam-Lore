What is DAM Lore?

DAM Lore provides users a few features that makes it simple and fun to explore the OSU and Corvallis Areas. The features included in DAM Lore are:


The Lore Page

Our collection of entries we thought every student and parent should see when they're in town.


The Map Page

An easy way to find all locations you may want to visit from our entries. It includes a serach bar, a custom map api, and an additional information section.


The Itinerary Page

Looking at all entries can be overwhelming. Luckily, we have provided you with just the solution. Our itinerary page allows the user to choose from a selection of tours like the campus or culture tour to guide their Corvallis experience.


The About and Mission Pages

These pages are dedicated to giving the user more information about our lovely team and a deeper explanation of our mission.


The Engagement Page

This project is from the students for the students, and with that we want to make sure it can evolve as the study body does. The engagement page focuses on collaboration with each student and user that visits this page and allows you, the user, to leave comments on potential areas of interest we may have missed.
